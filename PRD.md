# PRD: Post 010 — Hyperparameter Optimization

**AI Engineering Lab Series | Era 1: Classic Machine Learning**

## Objective
Teach Grid Search, Random Search, and Bayesian Optimization (Optuna) with clear comparison.

## Datasets
| Dataset | Domain | Rows | Task |
|---|---|---|---|
| 3D Printer Extrusion Quality | General (Manufacturing) | 5,000 | Maximize quality |
| PLL Loop Filter Tuning | Post-Silicon Validation | 5,000 | Minimize lock time |

## Concepts Covered
Grid search, random search, Bayesian optimization, Optuna, surrogate models, acquisition functions, cross-validation, parameter importance, search space visualization.
